Who is Kirk?

Kirk Burdett is a cool guy, and teacher at the interesting school: Hasland Hall Community School.
He also enjoys both rap and TED talks.

What does he teach?

With a degree in biology, Kirk is allowed to teach KS3 and GCSE Science, despite biology being a fake science. He is also a member of the notorious vigilante vape buster group known as SLT.

How do people feel about him?

Despite his meaningless qualification in a fake science, and his involvment with SLT, Kirk seems to have garnered quite a lot of clout amongst teachers, students and parents alike, with some students even saying they miss him.

What will the future be for Kirk?

With the imminent capture of Hasland Hall by Outwood Academy Trust, many people are concerned for the future of Kirk Burdett. Hopefully, they will decide to make the correct decision in keeping him on. Any other suggestion, in my opinion, would be stupid. However, if he is evicted, some would suggest he should return to his previous school, where all his best students are going.
